K-Lan

Check out my project on https://github.com/happy-t/K-Lan! It's all about creating a cool Chatting Application that works on Local Area Networks (LANs). What's really nifty is that it doesn't need the internet to chat – perfect for LAN parties or places with spotty Wi-Fi. I used Java, JavaScript, HTML, and CSS, with Apache Tomcat as the secret sauce. Imagine chatting with your buddies without the internet hassle – that's what K-Lan brings to the table. And the best part? It's open for others to join in and make it even cooler. Say goodbye to boring LAN chats – K-Lan's got your back!
